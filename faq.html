Daily Practice & Tasks

How do I use daily tasks?

Daily tasks are your core practice routine in Colocvial:

  1. Monologue: Practice free-form speaking on any topic
  2. Read Aloud: Improve articulation with provided texts
  3. Impromptu: Respond to spontaneous prompts to build quick-thinking skills

Each task is designed to target different aspects of conversational fluency. Complete all three for comprehensive daily practice.

Tip: Try to complete your daily tasks at the same time each day to build a consistent habit.
What if I miss a day?

No problem! Colocvial is designed to be flexible. The app focuses on weekly consistency rather than perfect daily streaks

Note: Progress tracking adapts to your schedule, so don't worry about occasional missed days.

Tags & Speech Analysis

How do I add tags to my recordings?

Tagging helps identify specific speech patterns you want to improve:

  1. After completing a recording, review the transcript
  2. Listen to your recording and tag the timestamp
  3. Choose from preset tags like Mumble or Stumble

Tags are tracked over time in your analytics dashboard, showing patterns and improvements.

What do the different tags mean?

Colocvial tracks several speech patterns:

  • Mumble: Unclear articulation or swallowed words
  • Stumble: Hesitations, filler words, or broken flow
  • Pace: Speaking too fast or too slow
  • Tone: Monotone delivery or inappropriate pitch
Tip: Start by tagging 2-3 common patterns. As you improve, focus on more specific areas.

Settings & Customization

How do I change the Read-Aloud language?

Colocvial currently supports multiple languages for read-aloud exercises:

  1. Go to Settings from the main menu
  2. Tap on Practice Language
  3. Select your preferred language from the list

Available languages include English, Romanian, Spanish

Note: Changing language affects the content of Read Aloud exercises and not the app language.

Progress Tracking

How does progress tracking work?

Colocvial tracks multiple metrics:

  • Weekly Trends: Visual charts showing improvement over time
  • Tag Frequency: How often specific patterns occur
  • Consistency Score: Based on regular practice

Access your analytics from the Dashboard Progress "View more" button to see detailed insights.

Why is my progress chart empty?

Progress charts need data to display. Make sure:

  • You've completed at least 3-4 recording sessions
  • You've added tags to your recordings

If you're new to Colocvial, complete a few days of practice first, then check your charts again.

Technical Support

The app isn't recording my voice

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Check microphone permissions in your device settings
  2. Ensure no other app is using the microphone
  3. Restart the Colocvial app
  4. Update to the latest version from the App Store

If issues persist, contact support at colocvial@mail.com
